Uber and other ride-share firms are not required to carry auto insurance under Georgia law.
under georgia law there is no requirement for these companies to carry insurance related to their business activities golick the chairman of the house judicial committee who works in the private sector as a regional attorney for allstate insurance company told the atlanta
put simply your personal auto insurance won t cover an incident when you re driving for uber or lyft whether when picking folks up or tooling around looking for fares
but insurance officials told the study committee that those individual policies don t apply once the contractors begin making money driving for the services
but golick said simply that georgia doesn t have any regulations on the firms at this point
the independent contractors who drive their own cars for the services are of course required to have personal insurance for their vehicles
